Data from R's datasets::gait (originally fda::gait), reshaped to long format. Source: Olshen, R. A., Biden, E. N., Wyatt, M. P., & Sutherland, D. (1989). Gait Analysis and the Bootstrap. Annals of Statistics 17(4), 1419–1440. Originally collected by the Motion Analysis Laboratory at Children's Hospital, San Diego.
